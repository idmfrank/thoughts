# Thoughts & Whitepapers

A simple, quick repository for collecting and sharing whitepapers and personal thoughts.

## Why this repo exists

- Keep ideas in one place.
- Share essays/whitepapers without heavy structure.
- Make it easy to add new thoughts quickly.

## Current documents

- `AI__and_Economic_Future.md` — A thesis arguing that AI-driven autonomous production is reshaping economics beyond the traditional capitalism vs. socialism framing, with control of infrastructure becoming the key axis.

- `When_Anti_Globalists_Started_Trusting_Global_Systems.md` — An essay on punk’s anti-centralization roots, globalization as integrated power, and why AI may force a centralization-vs-decentralization political divide.
